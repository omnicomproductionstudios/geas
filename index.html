Understanding Our Exam

The Global English Aptitude Solutions (GEAS) Exam is a comprehensive English proficiency test designed to assess real-world communication skills. It follows the CEFR (Common European Framework Of Reference for Languages), ensuring a structured and globally recognized evaluation of reading. writing. speaking. and grammar. The exam focuses on practical language use, critical thinking, and fluency, making it a reliable indicator of English proficiency for academic, professional, and personal growth. Candidates receive a certification valid for two years, helping them showcase their language skills to employers and institutions worldwide.
